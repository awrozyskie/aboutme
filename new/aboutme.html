Q & A with the Author


What are your favorite hobbies?

While technology reading and writing are my true loves of mine, everybody needs a mind clearing break. Over the past four years I have been an instructor teaching incoming freshman the Virginia Military Institute how to build team unity through overcoming both high and low initiative obstacles. In addition to this, I have also been working in the Writing Center as Manager of the Cadet E-portfolio team. In this hobby I help lead a team of seven tutors and we develop troubleshooting guides for cadets to use our campus WordPress program. I also enjoy the outdoors. I am a big fishing buff and rather enjoy hiking, kayaking, and even climbing. Chances are if it is outdoors I’m in.

What's your dream job?

My dream job is to work as a web developer for a business creating interactive websites that promote products and events, and can act as way for the company to interact with its customers. I would love to this for startup companies because I believe that communities are essential to local growth and development.
